Newbie Cardio: 3-Day Foundation

Build your cardiovascular base with three balanced sessions per week.

Day 1 of 3

Day 1: Light HIIT & Endurance Prep

  1. Dynamic warm-up — 5 min
  2. Jumping Jacks — 4×30s
  3. High Knees — 4×30s
  4. Plank — 3×45s
  5. Cool-down — 5 min
Day 2 of 3

Day 2: Steady State Cardio

  1. Warm-up walk — 5 min
  2. Jog — 20 min
  3. Row/Cycle — 10 min
  4. Cool-down — 5 min
Day 3 of 3

Day 3: Full Body Cardio Circuit

  1. Warm-up — 5 min
  2. Squats — 3×15
  3. Push-ups — 3×10
  4. Lunges — 3×10/leg
  5. Cool-down — 5 min
