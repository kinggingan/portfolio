Advanced Cardio: 9-Day Foundation

Challenge your peak performance with nine rotating, maximum-intensity sessions designed for high-performance athletes.

Day 1 of 9

Day 1: Tabata Max Power

  1. Warm-up: 5 min dynamic.
  2. Tabata (8 rounds/exercise): 20s Max Effort (Box Jumps), 10s Rest.
  3. Tabata 2: 20s Max Effort (Battle Ropes), 10s Rest.
  4. Core: V-ups 3x15.
  5. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 2 of 9

Day 2: VO2 Max Intervals

  1. Warm-up: 10 min easy.
  2. Intervals: 6 sets of 3 min at 90% max heart rate (fast run/row) followed by 3 min of easy recovery.
  3. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 3 of 9

Day 3: Active Recovery

  1. Low Intensity: 45-60 min light stretching
  2. Foam rolling
  3. Cycling at 50% max HR.
  4. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 4 of 9

Day 4: Circuit: Strength-Cardio Hybrid

  1. Warm-up: 5 min.
  2. Circuit (4 rounds, 2 min rest)
  3. Prowler Push (Max Effort 50m), Pull-ups (Max Reps), Box Jumps (15 reps).
  4. Farmer's Carry (1 min).
  5. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 5 of 9

Day 5: Steady State Challenge

  1. Warm-up: 5 min.
  2. Steady State: 45 minutes on an unconventional machine
  3. (Stairmaster or VersaClimber) at 75% max HR.
  4. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 6 of 9

Day 6: Sprinting & Plyometrics

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Sprints: 8 x 100m sprint (Max effort)
  3. 2-minute walk/jog recovery between each.
  4. Plyo: Broad Jumps 3x10.
  5. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 7 of 9

Day 7: Rest - Complete Off

  1. Complete rest. Zero planned activity.
  2. Focus on nutritional replenishment (high protein/carbs for muscle repair)
  3. Hydration (aim for 3-4 liters of water),
  4. Sleep optimization (aim for 8+ hours of uninterrupted sleep).
Day 8 of 9

Day 8: Long Duration Endurance

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Duration: 90 minutes of continuous, low-impact cardio (hiking, long swim, or cycling).
  3. Focus on fat-burning zone (60-70% max HR).
  4. Cool-down: 10 min mobility work.
Day 9 of 9

Day 9: Complex Finisher

  1. Warm-up: 5 min.
  2. EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute) for 20 minutes: Odd minutes.
  3. 10 Thrusters (light weight).
  4. Even minutes: 15 Burpees.
  5. Cool-down: 5 min.
