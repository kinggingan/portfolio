Intermediate Cardio: 6-Day Foundation

Enhance your endurance and fat-burning capacity with six structured, moderate-to-high intensity sessions per week.

Day 1 of 6

Day 1: Anaerobic Threshold Builder

  1. Warm-up: 5 min dynamic stretches.
  2. HIIT: 4 rounds of: 45s Hard Sprint/Bike + 90s Active Recovery Walk/Light Cycle.
  3. Core: 3 sets of 1-minute Plank.
  4. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 2 of 6

Day 2: Endurance Run/Cycle

  1. Warm-up: 5 min brisk walk/light cycle.
  2. Steady State: 35 minutes continuous effort (run, row, or cycle) maintaining 70-75% max heart rate.
  3. Cool-down: 5 min stretch.
Day 3 of 6

Day 3: Full Body Circuit Hybrid

  1. Warm-up: 5 min jump rope/jumping jacks.
  2. Circuit (3 rounds, 1 min rest between rounds)
  3. Burpees (10 reps), Kettlebell Swings (15 reps), Mountain Climbers (30 reps).
  4. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 4 of 6

Day 4: Active Recovery

  1. Low Intensity: 30-45 minutes of a low-impact activity
  2. Leisurely swim, yoga, or an easy walk
  3. Focus on mobility and blood flow.
Day 5 of 6

Day 5: Tempo & Pacing

  1. Warm-up: 5 min jog.
  2. Tempo: 3 sets of 1-mile run/row/cycle at a challenging but sustainable pace (8/10 effort).
  3. Rest for 3 min between sets.
  4. Cool-down: 5 min stretch.
Day 6 of 6

Day 6: Long Slow Distance (LSD)

  1. Warm-up: 5 min easy pace.
  2. LSD: 60 minutes of low-intensity walking or cycling outdoors.
  3. Focus on enjoying the process and building mental endurance.
  4. Cool-down: 5 min stretch.
