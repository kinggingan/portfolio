Intermediate Cycling: 6-Day Foundation

"Maximize your endurance and power-at-threshold with six targeted sessions per week."

Day 1 of 6

Day 1: Functional Threshold Power (FTP) Test Prep

  1. Warm-up: 10 min easy spin.
  2. Cadence Build-ups: 3 sets of 1 min gradually increasing RPM.
  3. High-Intensity Intervals: 5 x 1 min 'all-out' effort with 1 min recovery.
  4. Steady Spin: 15 min at 70% max heart rate.
  5. Cool-down & Stretching (5 min).
Day 2 of 6

Day 2: Hill Climb Simulation

  1. 1. Warm-up: 10 min easy.
  2. Climbs: 4 sets of 5 min high-resistance efforts (simulated climb) with 5 min easy spin recovery.
  3. Steady Pace: 20 min easy to flush legs.
  4. Cool-down (5 min).
Day 3 of 6

Day 3: Active Recovery & Mobility

  1. Low Resistance Cycle: 30 min at a very easy, conversational pace (Zone 1).
  2. Circuit (3 rounds, 1 min rest between rounds)
  3. Off-Bike: 15 min of Hip Flexor and Hamstring static stretching.
  4. Light Strength: 2 sets of 15 Bodyweight Squats.
  5. Cool-down (5 min).
Day 4 of 6

Day 4: VO2 Max Boost

  1. 1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Short Bursts: 8 x 30s sprint at maximum effort with 60s recovery spin.
  3. Steady Pace: 10 min easy spin.
  4. 5 min easy zone 1 ride.
  5. Cool-down: 5 min.
Day 5 of 6

Day 5: Long Sustained Ride

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Duration: 75 minutes continuous ride at 75-80% FTP (challenging but sustainable).
  3. Easy Spin: 5 min low resistance.
  4. Cool-down & Hydration (5 min).
Day 6 of 6

Day 6: Mixed Intervals & Endurance

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Mixed Intervals: 5 x 2 min high effort / 2 min recovery.
  3. Steady Pace: 15 min easy Zone 2.
  4. Strength: 3 sets of 15 Calf Raises.
  5. Cool-down (5 min).
