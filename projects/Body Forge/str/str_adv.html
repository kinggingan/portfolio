Advanced Strength Training: 9-Day Foundation

"Attain peak absolute strength and lean mass with nine intensive, rotating sessions."

Day 1 of 9

Day 1: Max Effort Lower Body (Squat Focus)

  1. Warm-up: 10 min spin + 5 min dynamic stretching.
  2. Low Bar Back Squat: 5 sets of 3 reps (RPE 8, max effort).
  3. Pause Squat: 3 sets of 5 reps.
  4. Leg Extension Superset w/ Leg Curl: 3 sets of 10-12 reps each.
  5. Core: Heavy Farmers Walk 3 sets of 40m & Cool-down Onwards.
Day 2 of 9

Day 2: Chest & Shoulders Volume

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Flat Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  3. Seated Dumbbell Overhead Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps.
  4. Cable Crossover (Low to High): 3 sets of 12-15 reps.
  5. Rotator Cuff Work: 3 sets of 15 external rotations & Cool-down 15 mins.
Day 3 of 9

Day 3: Back Thickness & Density

  1. Weighted Pull-ups: 4 sets of 5-8 reps.
  2. Pendlay Row: 4 sets of 6-8 reps (Strict form).
  3. Straight Arm Pulldown: 3 sets of 12-15 reps & Hyperextension (Weighted): 3 sets of 15 reps.
  4. Cool-down 20 mins.
Day 4 of 9

Day 4: Max Effort Upper Body (Bench Focus)

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Barbell Bench Press: 5 sets of 1-3 reps (RPE 9).
  3. Spoto Press (or Board Press): 3 sets of 5 reps.
  4. Triceps Skull Crushers: 4 sets of 8-10 reps.
  5. Cool-down: 15 min.
Day 5 of 9

Day 5: Rest & Deep Recovery

  1. Light Exercises and Stretching
  2. Complete Rest: Zero planned activity.
  3. Nutritional Replenishment.
  4. Sleep Optimization 8 hours.
Day 6 of 9

Day 6: Lower Body Hypertrophy & Hinge

  1. Warm-up: 10 min.
  2. Conventional Deadlift: 4 sets of 5 reps (Submaximal).
  3. Belt Squat or Hack Squat: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  4. Glute Ham Raise (GHR): 3 sets of 8-10 reps & Reverse Lunge (Dumbbells): 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
  5. Cool-down: 15 min.
Day 7 of 9

Day 7: Arms & Mobility

  1. Warm-up: 15 min.
  2. Bicep Curl (Alternating Dumbbell): 4 sets of 10-12 reps.
  3. Rope Triceps Extension: 4 sets of 10-12 reps.
  4. Forearm Work: 3 sets to failure & Mobility Circuit: 20 min focus on T-Spine and Hip Capsule.
  5. Cool-down and Rest: 15 min.
Day 8 of 9

Day 8: High Volume Full Body

  1. Warm-up: 5 min.
  2. Snatch-Grip Deadlift: 3 sets of 5 reps.
  3. Push Press: 3 sets of 8 reps & Landmine Row: 3 sets of 10 reps per side.
  4. Kettlebell Goblet Squat: 3 sets of 15 reps & Ab Wheel Rollout: 3 sets to max reps.
  5. Cool-down: 25 min.
Day 9 of 9

Day 9: Conditioning & Bodyweight Strength

  1. Warm-up: 15 min.
  2. Circuit (3 rounds, low rest): 10 Burpees, 15 Pull-ups, 20 Pistol Squats (or box assisted).
  3. Taper: 10 min easy cardio.
  4. Cool-down & Static Stretch (10 min).
